# Hubtify

**Your life, gamified.** Hubtify is a desktop app that turns your daily routines into an RPG adventure. Manage tasks, track nutrition, handle finances, and grow your character — all in one medieval-themed hub.

## Modules

### Questify — Task Management
Create quests (tasks) with due dates, subtasks, and priorities. Complete them to earn XP and level up your character. Track streaks, manage recurring quests, and watch your productivity grow.

### Nutrify — Nutrition Tracking
Log meals and get AI-powered calorie estimates. Track daily intake, close days to build consistency, and monitor your nutrition journey with charts and weekly balance stats.

### Coinify — Personal Finance
Full financial dashboard with transactions, recurring payments, installment tracking, credit card management, and loan tracking. Import bank statements and visualize your spending patterns.

### Cauldron — Focus Timer
Pomodoro-style timer with RPG flair. Configurable work/break presets, sound effects, and XP rewards for focused sessions. A floating global timer keeps you on track.

### Character — Your Avatar
Customize your RPG character. Level up by completing quests, logging meals, and managing finances. Your character reflects your real-life progress.

## Features

- **RPG Progression** — XP, levels, HP, combo multipliers, and random bonus rewards
- **Multi-Account Sync** — Cloud sync via Firebase across multiple accounts
- **Smart Notifications** — Context-aware reminders for overdue quests, unclosed nutrition days, upcoming installments
- **In-App Changelog** — See what's new in each version directly from settings
- **Auto-Updater** — Get notified and update seamlessly when a new version is available
- **Bilingual** — Full Spanish and English support
- **Medieval UI** — Parchment textures, Cinzel headers, hand-crafted SVG icons — no emojis

## Tech Stack

Electron 41 · React 19 · TypeScript · SQLite (better-sqlite3) · Firebase Firestore · Vite · GSAP
